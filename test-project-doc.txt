Project Name: E-Commerce Platform
Description: A modern e-commerce platform with product listings, shopping cart, and secure checkout.

Phases:
1. Discovery - Research and planning phase
2. Design - UI/UX design and architecture
3. Development - Core feature implementation
4. Testing - Quality assurance and bug fixing
5. Launch - Deployment and go-live

Personas:
- Sarah (Primary Buyer): Tech-savvy shopper who values fast checkout and product reviews
- Mike (Store Owner): Small business owner who needs an easy-to-use admin interface

Requirements:
- User authentication and authorization
- Product catalog with categories and search
- Shopping cart functionality
- Secure payment processing
- Order management system
- Responsive design for mobile and desktop
- Admin dashboard for product and order management
